Frequently Asked Questions

Q How much does it cost?

A To get the full experience of what it is like to have a Personal Concierge, we offer the service completely free for 1 month...no matter how many times you use it! After, your subscription can be handled on a monthly or annual basis. The monthly subscription is $9.99 and the annual subscription is $99.99.

Q How do I upgrade?

A Through the Connected Living app you can upgrade to gain access to 24/7/365 Personal Concierge support. To upgrade, you can touch anyplace on the app that says "Upgrade" or visit the "Explore" button on the bottom navigation.

Q How do I make a request from my Personal Concierge?

A Delegating a task is easy whether you are at work, at home or on the road. Through the Connected Living app you can call your Personal Concierge toll-free, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Q Who completes my request?

A Connected Living Personal Concierges handle all of the details of your requests. The Personal Concierges work together in teams to research your request and provide you with the best results. Personal Concierges have access to a wealth of resources to find the best possible solutions to meet your needs, including a proprietary knowledge base of expert resources, a network of preferred providers, unique research tools, and internal category specialists.

Q What type of information should I be prepared to provide?

A When you place a request with your Personal Concierge they will ask you a variety of questions that are tailored to your type of request. For example, if you are looking for a dining suggestion in New York City, your Personal Concierge will typically ask what type of cuisine, how many diners, and other pertinent questions. Any personal information you provide will only be used to complete your request.
